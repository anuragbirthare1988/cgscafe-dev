Job Description

  • Comfortable preparing café items using our guided blending methods and brand recipes.
  • Assist in preparing café favourites such as coffee, frappes, sandwiches and refreshing drinks like Mojito, while maintaining speed and consistency.
  • Bonus advantage if you have hands-on experience with baking items such as brownies, muffins, cupcakes, or cookies.
  • Help maintain cleanliness by regularly washing and organizing glassware, tableware, and kitchen utensils.
  • Follow basic hygiene and cleanliness practices to ensure a safe and pleasant café environment for everyone.
  • Interact with customers in a friendly and respectful manner to create positive and lasting experiences.
  • Support the team with billing and basic sales operations whenever required, along with food preparation tasks.
  • Basic English speaking ability is helpful for communicating comfortably with English-speaking customers.
Note: If needed, short training will be provided for 2-3 days to help you become familiar with our preparation methods and workflow.
