Our Approach

A Different Coffee Perspective

Most cafés build around espresso — bold, intense, and sharp.

At CGS, we chose a different path.

We use premium instant coffee as a base, not as a shortcut, but as a way to create a smoother, more approachable experience.

Why It Works

Not everyone enjoys bitterness or heavy intensity.

Our approach focuses on balance — allowing flavours like hazelnut, chocolate, or cinnamon to stand out clearly alongside coffee.

The result is a drink that feels lighter, smoother, and more enjoyable.

Precision Over Pressure

Instead of relying on machine pressure, we rely on precision.

Every measurement, texture, and blend is carefully controlled to ensure consistency.

This creates a refined experience across every visit.

Beyond Expectations
