The Journey

From quiet experiments to a thoughtfully built space

The Beginning

A Curious Start

It began with a simple curiosity — could a familiar cup of instant coffee be made smoother, more refined, and more enjoyable? What started as small experiments soon became a daily pursuit of getting it just right.

The Process

Refining the Craft

Night after night, recipes were adjusted, ratios were tested, and textures were refined. With carefully selected premium ingredients and a focused approach to instant coffee, each variation moved closer to something that felt complete.

The Shift

From Experiment to Idea

Over time, it became clear — this wasn’t just experimentation. It was a different way of experiencing coffee. One that felt smoother, more balanced, and quietly distinctive.

The Space

CGS Comes to Life

That idea eventually became CGS — a café in Indore designed to offer not just food and beverages, but a thoughtful experience where every detail reflects care, intention, and consistency.

Today

More Than Just a Café

Today, CGS is a space for those who appreciate balance over intensity and quality without unnecessary complexity — where every cup and every bite carries the same thoughtful approach it began with.
